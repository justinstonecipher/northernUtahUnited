Soccer Coach Stan Baker on What's Important For Youth Player Development

By: Diane Scavuzzo: Stan Baker talks about warming up the spirit of youth soccer players and remembering the joy in player development. What’s Baker’s focus? Increasing a players’ ability to make intelligent decisions, not just improve their technique. Soccer starts in the head (perception and decision making), flows through the heart, (passion for the game) and ends in the feet (the execution or technique). We have placed so much emphasis on the feet that we have forgotten where it all needs to start.
