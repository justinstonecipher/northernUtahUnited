How to Become a Competition Soccer Referee in Utah

All USSF affiliates, including the Utah Youth Soccer Association and the Utah Soccer Association, require that all participating referees be certified by the United States Soccer Federation (USSF). The Utah State Soccer Referee Committee (USSRC) is the governing body for Utah's USSF referees. It is the goal of this committee to actively recruit, develop, certify and advance referees for all levels of play.

The Utah Soccer Referee program offers two Entry Level clinics, the USSF Grade 8 Referee and Grade 9 Recreational Referee clinics, which are sponsored by affiliate organizations in various locations throughout the state. In order to become certified participants must attend one of the two courses. The courses differ in content and length. It is necessary to determine which clinic is more appropriate for age of the participant and/or the desired level of participation.

The Information for New Referees document provides more insight into what is expected and required once you become a certified referee.



For New Competition Referees

Once you successfully complete a referee course (see the course information below) and register with the USSF, you will receive your badge and will need to get appropriate referee gear, including a soccer referee uniform, soccer referee flags, soccer referee cards, whistles, watches and shoes.

Cost of the soccer referee uniforms vary but you can purchase good quality soccer referee uniforms for as little as $19.00 per referee jersey and $12 per referee shorts. You should also know that USSF approved five different colors of jerseys for its referees, including gold, black, blue, green and red. You should eventually purchase all five colors (you want to be able to change your jersey in the event one of the teams wears similar colors) but the gold referee jersey is considered standard and this is the one you should buy first.

You can also choose from three different soccer assistant referee flags, including checkered soccer referee flags, diamond pattern soccer referee flags and solid soccer referee flags. They all are acceptable and which ones you use are a matter of your personal taste and preference. Good quality soccer assistant referee flags will cost you between $8.00 to $15.00 for a pair.

With respect to soccer referee whistles, FOX 40 whistles are probably the best known and widely used referee whistles in the U.S but Acme whistles are a good choice as well. You can choose from multiple different designs such as classic or finger grip, with cushion mouth grip (CMG) or without. The cushion mouth grip whistles are ideal for those who hold their whistles in their mouths for extended periods of time. The most popular referee whistles on the market today are also pealess because they are the most reliable. Since there is no moving parts, these whistle will not freeze or jam and produce consistent, clear sound. At the very minimum, you should have two whistles with you at all time during your games. Depending on its design and its features, you should be prepared to spend between $3.00 and up to $10.00 on a good whistle.

There are also many different types of soccer referee cards to choose from. There are, for example, write-on referee cards and single replacement referee cards. These cards are economically priced and you should be able to purchase them for as little as $2.00 to $4.00 per set. Somewhat more expensive but very popular are soccer referee wallets. The wallets come with one set of referee cards and may also include a coin holder pocket or Report Form with Score Pads. There are also fully equipped Soccer Referee Kits and organizers that include cards, score pads, notebooks, and room for your whistles and pen. These organizers will help you keep all of your small referee equipment items in one easy-to-find place. The wallets are priced somewhere between $4.00 and $8.00 and organizers up to $20.00.

You will also have to get a soccer referee watch, preferably with a timer that has a stop/start function, to keep time of the game as well as wasted and added time. Some of the most popular referee watches are Champion Referee Watch and Robic Referee Watch. However, there are other sports watches such as those manufactured by Ohsen or O.T.S. that do the job as well. Their prices range from $15.00 to $33.00. You should always wear and keep the time of the game on two referee watches so that in case when one of them malfunctions the other shows the correct time.
