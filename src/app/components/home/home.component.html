Our Purpose

Here at Northern Utah United our purpose is to:

  • Provide youth of all ages a way to continue to grow their love for soccer.
  • Have the greatest competitive leagues and tournaments in northern Utah.
  • Keep everyone entertained with events for everyone in the family!
  • Ensure all youth have a fun time in our recreation leagues.
  • Help our youth learn to work as a team and gain leadership skills while they are young.

The most important part of Northern Utah United is to provide a wholesome experience for everyone, and ensure that everyone can gain experience.

We are always looking for volunteers here at Northern Utah United. If you, or anyone you know, wishes to volunteer with us, please contact us.
