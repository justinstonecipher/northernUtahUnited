As always, we take pride in our independent teams that participate in NUU. The coaches of these teams create a great experience and facilitate opportunities at various levels of play and training. We hope that the coaches and managers will help future teammates find the right fit for them by creating a team site to have posted here.

Last year NUU established the Sterling Program with the intent of having our Director of Coaching run training sessions and mentor each teams coaching staff both on and off of the field. The Sterling Program will continue with a larger staff and a more favorable cost per player arrangement. It will also include a coach to coach mentoring program that will challenge the 2018-2019 Sterling Coaches to mentor a new 2019-2020 Sterling coaching staff. The mentoring portion of the Sterling Program is optional for each coaching staff to take advantage of or not. Click here to see the training package options and costs for the Sterling Program.

In 2019-2020 we are introducing the NUU “Royalty Program.” This program is for the most ambitious teams in Northern Utah United ages 10-16. For a team to become a Royalty team they must apply by filling out an online survey by May 19th 2019. The Royalty teams will consist of like-minded coaches that will collaborate in training and reflection while assisting one anothers teams. There will be a common system of play to create consistency and continuity among players and coaches. Every royalty team gets to keep their own team name, identity and jersey. They will be identified as royalty teams in training with training shirts and on game days with armbands that reflect their “royalty” status in NUU. This is not a program for everyone. This is a select program for the most ambitious of coaching staffs and their teams. The Royalty teams should be viewed as the ultimate team to strive to play for in the club if a player has ambitions to play beyond High School, as they will attend more tournaments, train more frequently and work to earn starting spots and playing time among other ambitious players while being guided by sincere coaches that are dedicated and care for the individuals on the team as much as he/she cares for the team to be successful in player development and competition. Royalty teams will remain in their current Jerseys and represent their independence or affiliation with other teams that share the same name. Click here to apply to be a Royalty Team. If you are interested in learning more about the Royalty Program please email Coach Ginn (NUUginn@gmail.com).
