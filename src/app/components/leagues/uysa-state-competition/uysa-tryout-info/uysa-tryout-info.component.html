UYSA League Tryout Info

State Comp League tryouts for all U9 & U10 teams only, may begin the week before Memorial day – beginning on May 23rd, 2016.

All other Age groups may not begin until the Tuesday, following Memorial day – May 31st, 2016.

Registration will open on Monday, June 6th and the NUU Registration deadline will be on Friday, June 24th, 2016.

If you are wanting to start a team, please go to the resources page under downloads and look for registration instructions. Then please fill out an ad regarding your tryout dates and times, on the tryout page.

Make sure to review the "Registration Info" page thouroughly including the new age configurations for this 2016/17 season.
