Spring Boys High School Challenger League

At Northern Utah United we believe it is important that all youth have a place to play in our community. For many years we have had the challenge of providing a local experience for players not participating in their High School program. These players that want to play during their High School season have had to create provisional teams that travel all over the state for games. We think that there is a better solution, one that allows players to compete in our community while representing their high schools.

Northern Utah United is offering players U14-U18 an opportunity to play in a 7vs7 League where each high school will have an upper-classmen team (U17-19, players born: 2002-2004) and a lower-classmen team (U14-U16, players born: 2005-2007). Playing small sided games (7v7 or 9v9) locally will not only benefit the kids, but the high school programs as well; as it provides more potential touches per player, which is not only more fun for players, but also creates better development of skills.

If a particular High School has enough players to form two teams at either age group that will be accommodated.

Each team will be able to select their own home field and have the option to identify the Real Salt Lake Indoor Center as their home field. We want this to be both convenient and exciting for those that are participating.

These teams will have no official affiliation with their respected high schools, but their jersey will carry the mascots name. All of the Coaches from the following high schools are aware of this program and support it: Preston HS, Box Elder HS, Bear River HS, Sky View HS, Green Canyon HS, Ridgeline HS, and Mountain Crest HS. Each team will need to have a coach, who will be required to take some online coaching courses and legally mandatory safety courses.

$100 Per Player: The cost will cover field fees, league fees, insurance and a soccer shirt for game days. Fees can be paid over two installments of $50 each. Registration fees do not cover the cost of Referee fees. Teams will have to be prepared to collect and pay referee fees in the amount of $55 per game through VENMO.

If a player is already registered for a club team that is competing during the high school season, they may still participate in this league, with the permission of their club coach. We hope to have a 7-10 game season. The games will depend on how many teams we sign up.

Players on the under-classmen team will be able to play up with the upper-classmen if wanted/needed.

Please register for the team that best represents your high school so we can group all of the people in your area together.
