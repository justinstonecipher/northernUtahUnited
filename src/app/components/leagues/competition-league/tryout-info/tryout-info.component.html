Challenger League Tryout Info

Challenger League tryouts for all U9 & U10 teams only, may begin the week before Memorial day – beginning on May 22nd, 2017.

All other Age groups may not begin until the Tuesday, following Memorial day – May 30th, 2017.

Registration will open on Monday, June 5th and the NUU Registration deadline will be on Friday, June 23rd, 2017.

If you are wanting to start a team, please go to the resources page under downloads and look for registration instructions. Then please fill out an ad regarding your tryout dates and times, on the tryout page.

Make sure to review the "Registration Info" page thouroughly.

The Challenger League will follow the same Tryout guidelines as the state competition program. For more information go to section 6316 of the UYSA state bylaws and policies, here
