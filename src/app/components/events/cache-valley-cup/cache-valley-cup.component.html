Mark your calendars and plan to join us on July 13th - July 16th for the 12th Annual Cache Valley Cup Soccer Tournament, sponsored by Northern Utah United youth soccer. Join the tradition of excellence and fun that the Cache Valley Cup is known for.

Play your guaranteed three games against teams from the 'Intermountain West' on fields adjacent to the Cache National Forest in the shadow of the Bear River Range of the Wasatch Mountains.

This year, due to last minute construction at the Sky View Stadium, the Carnival and Friday night festivities have been moved from Sky View HS to Logan HS.

Also, due to that same construction – all games originally scheduled on Sky View Field Number - #7351 will be moved to the field on the NE corner of Mt. Logan Middle School – Field #5

When: July 13th - 16th, 2016
Registration Deadline: June 19th
Ages: U9-U18
Location: Cache Valley
Fields: various Cache Valley Locations (see the Fields/Venues for the details)
Fees:
  • U9-U10 - $450.00
  • U11-U12 - $475.00
  • U13-U18 - $550.00
