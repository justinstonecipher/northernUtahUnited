Mark your calendars and plan to join us on July 10th - July 13th for the 15th Annual Cache Valley Cup Soccer Tournament, sponsored by Northern Utah United youth soccer. Join the tradition of excellence and fun that the Cache Valley Cup is known for.

Play your guaranteed three games against teams from the 'Intermountain West' on fields adjacent to the Cache National Forest in the shadow of the Bear River Range of the Wasatch Mountains.
