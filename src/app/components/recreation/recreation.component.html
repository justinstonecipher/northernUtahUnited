Northern Utah United Youth Soccer offers an established successful recreation program, currently operating within Cache Valley from Logan north to the Idaho border.
This program promotes small-sided soccer for younger age groups, allowing for more active play. This program is designed with an emphasis on participation based on the philosophy of equal play-time at all positions, for all players, regardless of skill or experience, in accordance with US Youth Soccer's recreational philosophy.
