Thank You NUU Families for your support of the NUU Recreation Soccer Program for the past 30+ years. It has been our passion and our privilege to be a part of the lives of your youth players.
Our goal has always been to do what's best for kids in regards to soccer programming here in Cache Valley. In the last year, Northern Utah United has evolved the mission of our organization to try to meet the growing demands for more technical development of our players. With this end goal in mind, we currently run several programs for non-competitive players such as Destination Soccer & Royalty Prep, that are designed help players develop skills to build confidence and success at a young age before moving onto more competitive soccer programs.
Destination Soccer has served over 1,200 kids since its inception in late 2018. The enrollment for Destination soccer is year round, in four week sessions, allowing parents greater flexibility to choose the season that works best for their families.
For those that continue to want a 'traditional recreational soccer' experience, we encourage you to reach out to the various city recreational programs around the valley.
