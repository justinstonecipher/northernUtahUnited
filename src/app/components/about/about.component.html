Youth Soccer at it's finest

Because the sport is fun even with a beginner's skill level, Northern Utah has embraced Soccer in record numbers. Even better it's a game you can play and enjoy for the rest of your life! With this lifelong enjoyment as a focal point Northern Utah United (NUU) has become a leader in promoting, developing and governing youth soccer in Northern Utah; providing quality educational and developmental opportunities for players and coaches alike.

Everybody likes to win, but developing successful players and youth is what it is all about. We are a soccer organization with happy, healthy kids as our passion and priority. Every decision, every rule and every program has "what's good for the kids" as its basis. Every girl and boy stepping on a soccer field for the first time needs to have fun.

With over 20 years of experience organizing youth soccer at all levels of play in Cache and Box Elder counties we have dedicated ourselves to providing the best possible youth soccer programs with our UYSA Sanctioned Recreation and Competitive Soccer or Challenger Leagues each with age appropriate small-sided to full size 11 v 11 games.

As a non-profit organization, we are excited for the future of soccer in our community. We make every stride to share the same mission statement that our national governing body does. “US Youth Soccer is non-profit and educational organization whose mission is to foster the physical, mental and emotional growth and development of America's youth through the sport of soccer at all levels of age and competition.”
